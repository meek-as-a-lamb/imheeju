Heeju explores modes of sensory connection through sound and the conditions that constitute them. By tuning the interstitial conditions between sounds, she experiments with how relations can begin and take shape within them. For Heeju, sound is a painstaking gesture toward the unsayable, born of a longing for beings that, beyond the bounds of ethics and morality, bring noise into being. In 2024, she released an album under the name Vegetable Wife.
